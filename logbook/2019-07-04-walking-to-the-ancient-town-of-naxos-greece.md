On the north west shore of Naxos Island is the Naxos Marina provides a great place to anchor and spend the night with anchorages, moorings and even stern to dock based on availability.

We took the dinghy to shore so we could explore the Ancient town, grab a nice meal and even go visit the nearby Temple of Apollo.
