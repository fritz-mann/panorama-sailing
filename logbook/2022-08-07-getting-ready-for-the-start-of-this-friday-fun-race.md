As part of our sailing culture at the Royal St. Lawrence Yacht Club, we always invite adult sailing students from all levels, to participate as crew in the weekly club sailing races and regattas. 

Many of our graduates enjoy coming back to crew on our member's boats every week, as we see them here preparing for the start of this week's Friday Fun Race.
