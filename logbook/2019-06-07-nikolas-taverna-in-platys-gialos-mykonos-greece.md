We kept coming back to Nikolas Taverna during the whole time we were in Mykonos not only for the food but the scenery...I mean check out that view right ?\
\
The tables are  right on the beach (Agia Anna Beach ) and according to the local experts, this traditional taverna has been run by the same family for three generations, beginning in 1967. 

Most of the fish is caught by the owner, Nikolas, and his son and most of the meat and vegetables come from the family farm. Perfect spot for a meal while making the walk along the dirt path between the beaches of the southern coast. https://santorinidave.com/mykonos-restaurant-nikolas-taverna
