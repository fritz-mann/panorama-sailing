Sailing from Alyko Beach in Naxos to Ann Koufonisi is roughly 13-14 nautical miles if you're lucky with the wind but we decided to make a pit stop at Ann Koufonisi marina to replenish our fresh water before heading back out to find a nice anchorage in Pori Beach Bay further up the coast.

Pori Beach Bay was absolutely beautiful and the perfect location to visit the nearby sights of Ksylompatis Caves and the Devil's Eye swimming whole.
