These skies have inspired many artists. 

Safety is a critical part of this course and since the forecast was calling for winds of 12-15 knots gusting 18-22 knots, it was the necessary to use a jib instead of a genoa and putting a reef in the main sail, before leaving the dock.

Here we see the students getting the boats ready to head out for tonight’s lesson on choosing the right sails, reefing early and preparing for strong winds and gusts.
