Amazing view of the caldera from the old town of Thira in Santorini. Impossible to anchor here because of how deep the caldera is so if you are lucky enough to get a mooring buoy, take it and dinghy to the jetty in Santorini Old Port.

A better option if you want to spend the night is finding a mooring across the  bay on the southern shore of New Kammeni Islet.

The town is overrun by tourists and cruise ships so get there extremely early if you want to enjoy it without too many crowds.
