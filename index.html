About Me

A Life Defined
by Freedom

I gave up working in tech for something better — sailing and traveling to explore our world. I hope my posts and stories inspire you to discover for yourself why so many of us are passionate about sailing, and the freedom it brings.

After 15 years of sailing for pleasure, I became a certified sailing instructor and skipper for hire. Whether I'm taking you out on the water, teaching first-time sailors, or serving as your captain aboard a chartered sailing yacht — my goal is the same: an unforgettable experience that stays with you long after you've left the dock.
